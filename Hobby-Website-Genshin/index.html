  • About Genshin Impact

  • -Genshin Impact is an action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo. Genshin Impact was released on 28 September 2020, and has gone on as one of the most popular new games.

  • -The game includes voice-overs in four languages (English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean), and 13 different languages for text.

  • -The game features an open world environment and action-based battle system using elemental magic and character-switching. You can have up to 4 characters in your team. During fights, you constantly cycle between members of your team and stack their abilities to customize attacks against monsters.

  • -As your characters level up, you can level the world up too — getting tougher monsters and better rewards once you defeat them.


  • Background Setting



  • -Genshin Impact takes place in the world of Teyvat, and is composed of seven major nations being Mondstadt, Liyue, Inazuma, Sumeru, Fontaine, Natlan, and Snezhnaya, each ruled by a god.

  • -Each nation is associated with and worships one of The Seven, a group of gods known as Archons who each preside over one of the seven nations.

  • -Each characters have control over one of seven natural elements: Cryo, Dendro, Pyro, Hydro, Anemo, Electro and Geo, which correspond to ice, nature, fire, water, air, electricity, and earth.

  • The Vision:
  • “For to be stripped of one’s Vision is to be stripped of one’s ambition.”
  • ---Yae Miko

  • -A Vision is a gift bestowed upon those who dwell in Teyvat and are recognized by the Gods. They grant the user elemental abilities based on the element of their given Vision.

  • -It is said that those with Visions have a chance of ascending to Celestia [the residence of the gods] and attaining godhood themselves.

  • -Visions cannot be destroyed, and after a Vision wielder's passing, their Vision will deactivate, losing its color and rendering it unusable unless it is reactivated.


  • Storyline/Lore

  • -A pair of twins who travel across the stars and visit different worlds arrive in the land of Teyvat, when they wanted to leave, the Unknown God blocked their way and started a fight with them. In the end, the Unknown God took one of the siblings and sealed away the other.

  • -A long time passes, the sealed twin, referred to as the "Traveler", awakens and meets Paimon. The pair travel around the Seven Nation to start their search for the Traveler's missing twin.

  • -The story of a lost nation starts to appear, all the truths are waiting to be discovered…
