    Adventure EXP

    【Open World Game-play:】

    • Adventure Rank/World Level:

    • -Adventure Rank (AR) is an account-wide progression level that is raised through Adventure EXP.

    • -Upon reaching certain Adventure Ranks, players will be able to ascend their world to the next World Level, either automatically or by completing an Ascension Quest.

    • -When a player's World Level increases, it increases the difficulty and rewards of Monsters, Bosses, Ley Lines, and Domains.


    [Common Chest --> Exquisite Chest --> Precious Chest --> Luxurious Chest]

    • 【Chests】

    • “Let’s see… What goods are inside?”

    • Players can find Chests throughout the whole Tevyat. Some which you can open directly, some are hidden which you can only get after completing puzzles.

    • Common Chest: Can be found just about anywhere in Teyvat. Some require the player to defeat monsters near it to unlock the chest.

    • Exquisite Chest: Are usually locked and are unlocked by fighting nearby monsters or solving puzzles. Gives you better rewards than common chests and a bit harder to find.

    • Precious Chests: Can only be found in very well hidden spots throughout the world or during quests.

    • Luxurious Chest: Very, very rare chests, can only be unlocked in hidden spots throughout the world. Only a very small amount of them. But it gives you great rewards.
